Trip to Flight Museum

The Museum of Flight is a private non-profit air and space museum in the northwest United States. It is located at King County International Airport, in the city of Tukwila, Washington, south of downtown Seattle. It was established in 1965 and is fully accredited by the American Alliance of Museums. As the largest private air and space museum in the world, it also hosts the largest K-12 educational programs in the world.
